SCSS Shaders: 3D Effects for the Web

Adobe, Apple and Opera jointly developed the CSS Shaders specification to generate high-quality video effects in the browser, and filters can be applied to any HTML element. They look familiar to anyone who has applied effects in a Flash animation editor, or to those who know the filter element from the Filter Effects 1.0 standard for SVG. It's the same here, only much better.

There are only nine preset effects for filter in the specifications

  1. blur(5, 5)
  2. drop-shadow(10, 5, 5)
  3. hue-rotate(328deg)
  4. saturate(5)
  5. invert(1)
  6. igrayscale(1)
  7. opacity(0.5)
  8. gamma(1.1, 3.6, 0)
  9. sepia(0.5)
